## App #2: AI Life Simulator (BitLife Killer)

### Concept
A text-based life simulator where every life event, NPC interaction, and story branch is generated by an on-device LLM — making every playthrough genuinely unique. BitLife uses scripted random events; this uses real AI.

### Market
- BitLife earns ~$1-2M/month, 600K downloads/month
- No life simulator uses real AI yet — completely untapped
- Text-based = perfect for LLM generation

### AI Role
- Generate unique life events based on your choices and history
- NPC conversations that feel real (boss, partner, friends, rivals)
- Branching storylines that remember everything you've done
- Personality system that evolves based on decisions

### Revenue Model
- Free: play through childhood + early adulthood
- Premium unlock: $4.99 one-time (full life)
- Cosmetic IAP: character customization, special scenarios
- Expansion packs: $1.99-2.99 each (celebrity life, crime boss, time travel)

### Key Differentiator
"Every life is unique" — powered by AI, not scripted random number generators. The AI remembers your entire life story and generates events that reference your past.

### Technical Notes
- Same MLX + Gemma/Qwen stack
- Lighter than Celestia (no ephemeris needed)
- Structured JSON output for life events (similar to DollBrain pattern)
- SwiftData stores entire life history for AI memory injection

### Estimated Build Time: 6-8 weeks

## App #3: Private AI Journal (Mental Health Companion)

### Concept
A journaling app with an AI therapist that uses CBT/DBT techniques — all 100% on-device. Your most intimate thoughts never leave your phone.

### Market
- Mental health apps are the fastest-growing app category
- AI chat apps exposed 300M+ messages in data breaches (2026)
- No dominant on-device private journal exists
- Users pay $20/mo for ChatGPT partly for emotional support

### AI Role
- Guided journaling prompts (CBT thought records, gratitude, reflection)
- Empathetic responses to journal entries
- Pattern detection across entries (mood trends, recurring themes)
- Gentle therapeutic techniques (cognitive reframing, grounding exercises)

### Revenue Model
- Free: basic journaling + 3 AI responses/day
- Premium: $9.99/month or $49.99/year — unlimited AI, mood tracking, insights reports
- One-time: $29.99 lifetime option

### Key Differentiator
"Your therapist journal that literally cannot leak your data because it never leaves your phone." The privacy story is the entire marketing angle.

### Technical Notes
- Simplest of all 5 apps to build (no game mechanics, no ephemeris, no card systems)
- System prompts loaded with CBT/DBT frameworks
- Mood tracking via structured JSON output (mood score, themes, triggers)
- SwiftData stores journal entries for long-term pattern analysis

### Estimated Build Time: 4-5 weeks

## App #4: AI Food/Calorie Tracker (MyFitnessPal Disruptor)

### Concept
Point your camera at food → AI identifies it and estimates calories/macros. Log meals with natural language ("I had a turkey sandwich and an apple"). All on-device, no subscription needed.

### Market
- MyFitnessPal: 200M+ users, 1.5-star Trustpilot rating, $80-100/year premium
- Every AI food tracker uses cloud processing
- Health data is extremely sensitive — privacy pitch is strong

### AI Role
- Vision model (Gemma 3n multimodal) identifies food from camera
- LLM parses natural language food descriptions into nutritional data
- Meal suggestions based on dietary goals and history
- Weekly nutrition insights and trend analysis

### Revenue Model
- Free: unlimited food logging + basic tracking
- Premium: $4.99/month or $29.99/year — detailed insights, meal planning, macro targets
- No ads ever

### Key Differentiator
Works offline (at restaurants, gyms, traveling). No subscription wall on basic scanning. Health data never leaves device.

### Technical Notes
- Requires multimodal model (Gemma 3n supports image input)
- Needs nutritional database bundled locally (USDA FoodData Central is public domain)
- More complex than journal but simpler than Celestia
- Camera integration via AVFoundation

### Estimated Build Time: 8-10 weeks

### Risk
Nutritional estimation accuracy with a small vision model. May need user confirmation/correction flow to maintain trust.

## App #5: AI Interactive Fiction (AI Dungeon Replacement)

### Concept
Choose-your-own-adventure stories generated by on-device AI. Pick a genre (fantasy, sci-fi, horror, romance), set the scene, and the AI writes a branching story that responds to your choices. Your stories are private — no one reads them.

### Market
- AI Dungeon collapsed (traffic down 60%+, removed from Steam)
- NovelAI charges $10-25/month
- Privacy is a real concern — people write very personal fantasy content
- Loyal niche audience willing to pay

### AI Role
- Generate story narrative (2nd person or 3rd person)
- Maintain plot coherence within a session
- Respond to player actions with creative consequences
- Character dialogue and world-building

### Revenue Model
- Free: 3 stories/day with basic genres
- Premium: $4.99/month — unlimited stories, all genres, save/continue
- Story pack IAP: $1.99 each — themed adventures (detective noir, space opera, etc.)

### Key Differentiator
Private stories that never leave your device. No content filter surprises (AI Dungeon's filter destroyed stories mid-flow). Works offline.

### Technical Notes
- Pure LLM task — no vision, no external libraries needed
- Needs careful prompt engineering for plot coherence
- Session-based stories fit well within context window limitations
- Could support a "kids mode" with age-appropriate content filtering (reuse ContentFilter from Mochi Crew)

### Estimated Build Time: 5-7 weeks
